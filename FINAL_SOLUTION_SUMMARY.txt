### Trajectoire Pick-Place
```
HOME (initial)
  ↓ 3.0s
APPROCHE_PICK (510mm, au-dessus objet)
  ↓ 3.0s
PICK (saisir l'objet)
  ↓ 3.0s
LIFT (soulever 10cm)
  ↓ 3.0s
APPROCHE_PLACE (400mm, vers zone drop)
  ↓ 3.0s
PLACE (poser l'objet)
  ↓ 3.0s
RETRAIT (dégager)
  ↓ 3.0s
HOME (repos)

TOTAL: 8 mouvements × 3s + pauses = ~27 secondes
```
